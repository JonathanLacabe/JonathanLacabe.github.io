Summary of Electromagnetism

Prerequisites:

Physics:
-Classical Mechanics

Math:
-Single-Variable Calculus.
-Multivariable Calculus is occasionally referenced (for the surface integral in the Charge-Flux Law (Gauss's Law)), though knowledge of the subject is not required (and is indeed explained where referenced).

These are my complete notes for Electromagnetism, covering such topics as Electric Charge, the Law of Electric Force, Conduction/Induction, Electric Fields, Electric Flux, Circuitry, Ohm's Law, Magnetic Flux, Resistance, and more.

I color-coded my notes according to their meaning - for a complete reference of each type of note, see here (also available in the sidebar). All of the knowledge present in these notes has been filtered through my personal explanations for them, the result of my attempts to understand and study them from my classes and online courses. In the unlikely event there are any egregious errors, contact me at jdlacabe@gmail.com.
